Privacy Policy

Application: Gia · Effective Date: May 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ALRG QA Inc (“ALRG”, “we”, “our”) handles data in connection with its internal-use software application Gia (the “Application”).

1. Scope

The Application is operated exclusively for ALRG’s internal business purposes. It is not made available to the general public, is not distributed through any application marketplace, and does not provide services to data subjects outside of ALRG personnel and ALRG’s own customer records.

2. Data We Access

The Application connects, through authorized API credentials, to the following systems owned or licensed by ALRG:

  • Intuit QuickBooks Online — ALRG’s accounting data, including chart of accounts, transactions, bills, invoices, customers, vendors, and reports.
  • Google Workspace — ALRG’s Gmail, Drive, and Calendar data.
  • Slack — ALRG’s internal workspace channels and messages.
  • Follow Up Boss — ALRG’s real-estate CRM contact and transaction data.
  • Other third-party services explicitly authorized by ALRG in the course of its real-estate brokerage operations.

All data accessed by the Application originates from systems where ALRG is the account owner and the data controller.

3. How We Use Data

Data accessed by the Application is used solely for the following internal business purposes:

  • Financial reporting, reconciliation, and bookkeeping accuracy checks;
  • Real-estate transaction coordination and pipeline tracking;
  • Customer relationship management and follow-up;
  • Internal performance analytics and operational dashboards;
  • Compliance and recordkeeping.

4. Storage and Security

API credentials and the data fetched by the Application are stored on infrastructure controlled by ALRG. Specifically:

  • OAuth credentials are stored in the Windows DPAPI secure vault on a dedicated ALRG host, encrypted under the host’s machine key.
  • Cached data is stored on ALRG’s own servers, with access restricted to authorized ALRG personnel.
  • Network transport to and from third-party APIs uses TLS encryption.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

ALRG does not sell, rent, or share data accessed by the Application with third parties for marketing or any other purpose outside of the internal business purposes described above. Data may be disclosed only:

  • To service providers strictly necessary for the operation of the Application (e.g., the third-party APIs from which the data was fetched);
  • As required to comply with applicable law, regulation, or valid legal process;
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of ALRG, its personnel, or customers.

6. Retention

Data is retained only for as long as needed to fulfill the business purposes described in this Policy and to satisfy applicable recordkeeping requirements. Cached data is regularly pruned. Credentials are rotated as required by the third-party services.

7. Your Rights

Because the Application processes data on behalf of ALRG, data subjects whose information appears within ALRG’s own records may exercise their rights through the underlying systems of record (QuickBooks Online, Follow Up Boss, etc.) or by contacting ALRG directly at the address below.

8. Children

The Application is not directed to children under 16 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

ALRG may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective Date” at the top of this document indicates when it was last updated.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact: Lukasz@ALRealtyGroup.com.
